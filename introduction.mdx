# Welcome to Crepes & Waffles Coworking

A modern coworking space management system designed to streamline desk reservations, attendance verification, and workspace administration with real-time synchronization.

## What is Crepes & Waffles Coworking?

Crepes & Waffles Coworking is a comprehensive SaaS platform built to manage coworking spaces efficiently. It provides an intuitive interface for users to reserve desks, confirm attendance via geolocation, and manage their bookings—all in real-time.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Real-Time Desk Reservations" icon="calendar-check">
    Book desks instantly with WebSocket-powered synchronization that keeps all users updated in real-time. See availability changes as they happen and avoid booking conflicts.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Geolocation-Based Attendance" icon="location-dot">
    Confirm your attendance automatically when you arrive at the coworking space. The system verifies your location within a 100-meter radius to ensure you're physically present.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Multi-Shift Scheduling" icon="clock">
    Choose from morning (8 AM - 12 PM), afternoon (1 PM - 5 PM), or full-day shifts. Flexible scheduling adapts to your work style and availability.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Desk Rotation Policy" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Automated rotation enforcement ensures fair desk distribution. The system prevents users from booking the same desk consecutively, promoting workspace variety.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Visual Desk Selection" icon="chair">
    Interactive visual interface shows desk availability at a glance. Click on desks in the workspace layout to see details and make reservations.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Admin Dashboard" icon="gauge-high">
    Comprehensive management tools for administrators to oversee all reservations, manually confirm bookings, and configure workspace settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Authenticate">
    Users log in with their employee credentials (document number/ID).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Available Desks">
    View the workspace layout showing which desks are available for your chosen date and time slot.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Make a Reservation">
    Select your preferred desk and time slot. The system validates desk rotation rules and availability in real-time.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Attendance">
    On the day of your reservation, confirm your attendance when you arrive. The system uses geolocation to verify you're at the workspace.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manage Your Bookings">
    View, modify, or cancel your reservations from the user panel. Admins can manage all user reservations from the admin dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Technology Stack

The platform is built with modern web technologies:

- **Frontend**: React 19 with Vite for fast development and optimized builds
- **UI Framework**: Ant Design components with custom SCSS styling
- **Routing**: React Router for seamless navigation
- **Real-Time Sync**: WebSocket integration for live updates
- **Geolocation**: Browser Geolocation API for attendance verification
- **API Integration**: Axios for backend communication
