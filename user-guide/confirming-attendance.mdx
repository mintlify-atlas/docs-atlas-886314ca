## Overview

All reservations start with a **"Pendiente"** (Pending) status. To activate your reservation, you must **confirm your attendance** on the day of your booking using the geolocation verification system.

<Warning>
  **Time-Sensitive Action Required**
  
  You have a **25-minute confirmation window** starting from your shift's begin time. If you don't confirm within this window, your reservation will be **automatically canceled** with the reason "! Usuario no confirmo!".
</Warning>

## Confirmation Windows by Shift

| Shift | Start Time | Confirmation Window | Deadline |
|-------|------------|---------------------|----------|
| **Mañana (AM)** | 8:00 AM | 8:00 AM – 8:25 AM | 8:25 AM |
| **Tarde (PM)** | 1:00 PM | 1:00 PM – 1:25 PM | 1:25 PM |
| **Día completo** | 8:00 AM | 8:00 AM – 8:25 AM | 8:25 AM |

<Note>
  For **full day** reservations, you only need to confirm once during the morning window (8:00 AM – 8:25 AM).
</Note>

## Geolocation Requirements

The system verifies that you are **physically present** at the coworking space using your device's GPS location.

### Location Details

- **Workplace coordinates**: Crepe-Working 1 (4.74488, -74.04483)
- **Required proximity**: Within **1,000 meters** (1 km) of the workplace
- **Technology used**: GPS geolocation (Haversine formula for distance calculation)

<Tip>
  The system shows your current distance in the User Panel. For example: **"Estás dentro del perímetro (245 m)"** means you're 245 meters from the workplace and eligible to confirm.
</Tip>

## Step-by-Step: Confirming Your Reservation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Location Services">
    Before you can confirm, your device must allow location access.
    
    **On iOS (iPhone/iPad):**
    1. Open **Settings**
    2. Find and tap **Safari** (or your browser)
    3. Tap **Location**
    4. Select **"Permitir"** (Allow) or **"Permitir al usar la app"**
    5. Reload the page
    
    **On Android:**
    1. Open **Settings**
    2. Go to **Applications** or **Apps**
    3. Find your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
    4. Tap **Permissions**
    5. Tap **Location** and select **"Permitir"** (Allow)
    6. Reload the page
    
    **On Desktop:**
    1. Click the **padlock icon** (🔒) in the address bar
    2. Find **"Location"** in the menu
    3. Select **"Allow"** or **"Ask"**
    4. Reload the page and click **"Allow"** when prompted
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Your User Panel">
    Click the **Armchair icon** in the top navigation bar to open your panel ("Mis Reservas").
    
    You'll see:
    - Your profile information
    - Quick summary of reservations (Pendientes, Confirmadas, Canceladas)
    - **"Confirmación por ubicación"** section showing your current status
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Your Location Status">
    The User Panel displays your proximity status:
    
    ✅ **"Estás dentro del perímetro ([distance] m)"**
    - You are within range and can confirm
    - Distance shown in meters (e.g., 245 m)
    
    ⚠️ **"Para confirmar, debes estar dentro del perímetro de 1000 m"**
    - You are too far away
    - Move closer to the workplace and tap **"Actualizar ubicación"**
    
    <Tip>
      Click **"Actualizar ubicación"** (Update Location) to refresh your GPS position. This is useful if you just arrived at the workplace.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for Your Confirmation Window">
    Reservations can only be confirmed **on the day of the booking** and **within the 25-minute window**.
    
    If you try to confirm too early, you'll see:
    - **"La confirmación para este turno inicia a las [time]"**
    
    If you try after the deadline:
    - **"La ventana de confirmación terminó a las [time]"**
    
    The panel shows: **"Ventana activa: quedan [X] min"** when the window is open.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Your Reservation">
    Find your pending reservation in the list and click the **green "Confirmar"** button.
    
    **On Mobile:**
    1. Tap the reservation card to expand it
    2. Verify the desk, date, and shift details
    3. Tap the green **"Confirmar"** button
    
    **On Desktop:**
    1. Find the reservation row in the table
    2. Click the **"Confirmar"** button in the "Acción" column
    
    The system will:
    1. Verify you're within the time window
    2. Check your GPS location
    3. Calculate distance to the workplace
    4. Update the reservation status
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Confirmation">
    If successful, you'll see an alert:
    
    **"Asistencia confirmada. Distancia: [X] metros."**
    
    The reservation status changes from **"Pendiente"** to **"Confirmada"** with a green badge.
    
    The system saves verification details including:
    - Confirmation timestamp
    - Your GPS coordinates
    - Distance to workplace (in meters)
    - Verification type: `confirmacion-manual-geolocalizada`
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Confirmation States

Each reservation has one of three states:

### 🟡 Pendiente (Pending)

- **Initial state** after booking
- **Action required**: Confirm during the 25-minute window
- **Badge color**: Yellow/gold background
- **What happens**: Auto-cancels if not confirmed by deadline

### 🟢 Confirmada (Confirmed)

- **Final state** after successful verification
- **Badge color**: Green background
- **Cannot be changed**: Once confirmed, the reservation is locked
- **Verification message saved**: Shows confirmation timestamp and distance

### 🔴 Cancelada (Canceled)

- **Badge color**: Red background
- **Reasons for cancellation**:
  - Manual cancellation by user
  - Auto-cancellation: **"! Usuario no confirmo!"** (missed deadline)
  - Admin cancellation with custom reason

## Troubleshooting Location Issues

### "Permiso de ubicación denegado"

**Cause**: Location permission was denied.

**Solution**: Follow the instructions in Step 1 to enable location services, then reload the page.

---

### "Ubicación no disponible"

**Cause**: Your device cannot determine your location (no GPS signal, WiFi disabled).

**Solution**:
- Enable **GPS** on your device
- Connect to **WiFi** for better positioning
- Move to a location with better signal (near a window, outside)
- Ensure **Location Services** are enabled system-wide

---

### "Tiempo de espera agotado"

**Cause**: GPS took too long to respond (timeout after 15 seconds).

**Solution**:
- Check your internet connection
- Enable GPS/Location Services
- Tap **"Actualizar ubicación"** to retry
- Try again from a location with better signal

---

### "Estás a [X] metros del punto. Debes estar dentro de 1000 metros..."

**Cause**: You are more than 1 km away from the workplace.

**Solution**:
- **Physical solution**: Travel to the coworking space before confirming
- **Check distance**: The message shows your current distance
- **Update location**: After arriving, click "Actualizar ubicación"

---

### "La geolocalización no está soportada en este navegador"

**Cause**: Your browser is outdated or doesn't support geolocation.

**Solution**: Use a modern browser:
- ✅ Chrome (recommended)
- ✅ Firefox
- ✅ Safari
- ✅ Edge
- Update your browser to the latest version

## Admin Override

Administrators can confirm reservations **manually without location verification**:

- No time window restriction
- No location requirement
- Verification type: `confirmacion-admin-manual`
- Message saved: **"Reserva confirmada por administrador"**

<Note>
  If you're an admin, you'll see a note in your User Panel: **"Puedes confirmar manualmente cualquier reserva pendiente, incluso fuera de la ventana de 25 minutos."**
</Note>

## What Happens If You Don't Confirm?

If you don't confirm within the 25-minute window:

1. **Auto-cancellation** runs after the deadline
2. Status changes to **"Cancelada"**
3. Cancellation reason: **"! Usuario no confirmo!"**
4. The desk becomes available for others
5. You can make a new reservation for a future date

<Warning>
  There is **no grace period**. The 25-minute window is strict. For example, if your shift starts at 8:00 AM, you must confirm by 8:25 AM exactly.
</Warning>

## Privacy and Data

The system stores the following information when you confirm:

- **Timestamp** of confirmation (ISO format)
- **GPS coordinates** (latitude, longitude)
- **Distance** to workplace in meters
- **Accuracy** of GPS reading (if available)
- **Verification type** (manual vs. admin)

This data is used **only** for attendance verification and is not shared externally.

## Tips for Smooth Confirmation

<Tip>
  **Arrive 10 minutes early** – This gives you time to enable location services, find a good GPS signal, and confirm comfortably within the window.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Check your location status beforehand** – Visit the User Panel 5 minutes before your shift to see if you're within range.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Enable GPS and WiFi** – Both improve location accuracy. GPS alone may be less accurate indoors.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Use a modern browser** – Chrome and Edge have the best geolocation support across devices.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Save your browser permissions** – Allow location access permanently to avoid re-enabling it each time.
</Tip>
