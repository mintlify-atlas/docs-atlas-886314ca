## Overview

The reservation system allows you to book a desk for specific time slots during business days. You can choose from 6 available desks, select your preferred shift (morning, afternoon, or full day), and view real-time availability.

## Before You Start

<Note>
  **Important Restrictions**
  - You can only make **one reservation per day**
  - Reservations between **12:00 AM and 5:00 AM** are restricted to tomorrow only (not day after tomorrow or later)
  - The desk rotation policy requires you to choose a **different desk** from your last reservation
</Note>

## Step-by-Step: Making a Reservation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Room">
    After logging in, you'll see available rooms. Click on a room card to continue.
    
    - Each card shows **capacity** (number of desks) and how many have monitors
    - Unavailable rooms show **"Próximamente"** (Coming Soon)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the Date">
    Use the date navigator at the top to choose between **today** or **tomorrow**.
    
    - Click the **left arrow** to go to the previous date
    - Click the **right arrow** to go to the next date
    - The current date is displayed in the center (e.g., "Lun. 15/3")
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Desk Availability">
    The visual map shows 6 desks around a central table. Each desk has a color-coded status:
    
    - **Green chair** = Available (all shifts free)
    - **Yellow chair** = Partially available (some shifts taken)
    - **Red chair** = Occupied (no shifts available)
    
    <Tip>
      Desks **1, 3, and 6** include a support monitor. Look for the monitor icon on the desk info panel.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Who's Reserved">
    On desktop, the **right panel** shows who has reserved each desk and their shift:
    
    - **"Escritorio 1"** to **"Escritorio 6"** are listed
    - Each person's photo (or avatar), first name, and shift badge (AM/PM/Todo el día) appear
    - On mobile, tap the **"Escritorios"** floating button at the bottom to see this information
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Your Desk">
    Click on an **available or partially available** desk (green or yellow chair).
    
    <Warning>
      If you see the error **"Tu última reserva fue el [date] en el escritorio [number]. Debes hacer rotación y elegir otro escritorio"**, you must select a **different desk** due to the rotation policy.
    </Warning>
    
    A booking card will appear on the right (or bottom on mobile) showing:
    - Your name, position, and area
    - The selected desk number
    - Whether it has a monitor
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your Shift">
    Select one of the available shifts by clicking the corresponding button:
    
    | Shift | Hours | Badge |
    |-------|-------|-------|
    | **Mañana** | 8:00 am – 12:00 m | AM |
    | **Tarde** | 1:00 pm – 5:00 pm | PM |
    | **Día completo** | 8:00 am – 5:00 pm | Todo el día |
    
    <Note>
      Blocked shifts appear dimmed and cannot be selected. This happens when:
      - Someone else already reserved that shift
      - You select "Día completo" (blocks both AM and PM)
      - The desk has both AM and PM reserved (blocks "Día completo")
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Your Reservation">
    Review the booking summary and click **"Reservar"** (Reserve).
    
    The button is **disabled** (grayed out) if:
    - You already have a reservation for this day: **"Ya tienes una reserva para este día"**
    - The current time is between 12:00 AM and 5:00 AM and you're booking beyond tomorrow: **"No puede reservar para pasado mañana (o fechas posteriores) entre 12:00 am y 5:00 am."**
    - The desk rotation policy blocks your selection
    - All shifts are taken: **"Este escritorio no tiene turnos disponibles"**
    - The system is validating rotation: **"Validando regla de rotación..."**
    
    When successful, you'll see **"Reservado!"** and the desk map will update automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding the Interface

### Top Navigation Bar

- **Date selector** – Navigate between business days
- **Armchair icon** – Opens your user panel ("Mis Reservas" or "Panel Admin" for admins)
- **Logout icon** – Sign out and return to login
- **Back arrow** – Return to previous screen

### Booking Card Sections

1. **"Reserva para"** – Shows your profile photo, name, position, and area
2. **"Ubicación"** – Displays the desk number and monitor availability
3. **"Horario"** – Lists the three shifts with availability status
4. **Feedback area** – Shows success messages, errors, or warnings
5. **Action buttons** – "Cancelar" (cancel selection) and "Reservar" (confirm)

### Legend (Bottom of Map)

- **Monitor icon** – "Con monitor: Esc. 1, 3 y 6"
- **Desk status icons** – Disponible (green), Limitado (yellow), Ocupado (red)

## Common Errors and Solutions

| Error Message | Meaning | Solution |
|---------------|---------|----------|
| **"Ya tienes una reserva para este día"** | You already have a reservation today | Cancel your existing reservation first or choose another date |
| **"Tu última reserva fue el [date] en el escritorio [number]..."** | Rotation policy active | Choose a different desk number |
| **"Este escritorio no tiene turnos disponibles"** | All shifts taken | Select another desk |
| **"No puede reservar para pasado mañana..."** | Early morning restriction | Wait until 5:00 AM or book for tomorrow only |
| **"Este turno ya no está disponible..."** | Shift just taken by someone else | Choose another shift or refresh the page |
| **"Este escritorio acaba de ser reservado por otro usuario"** | Desk became occupied during your selection | Choose another desk |

## Tips for Successful Reservations

<Tip>
  **Book early in the day** – Desks fill up quickly, especially those with monitors.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Check the rotation policy** – The system automatically enforces desk rotation to ensure fair access. Plan to use different desks each day.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Use the mobile drawer** – On mobile devices, tap the "Escritorios" button to see who's reserved each desk without leaving the map view.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Monitor availability updates in real-time** – The desk map refreshes automatically when new reservations are made.
</Tip>

## What's Next?

After making your reservation:

1. **Check your User Panel** to see your reservation details
2. **Prepare to confirm your attendance** within the 25-minute window on the day of your reservation
3. Review the [Confirming Attendance](/user-guide/confirming-attendance) guide to understand the geolocation requirements
