## Overview

The **desk rotation policy** is an automated rule that ensures fair and equitable access to all workspaces. The system enforces that you **must use a different desk** from your most recent reservation.

<Note>
  **Policy Goal**: Prevent desk monopolization and distribute access to popular desks (especially those with monitors) across all users.
</Note>

## How Rotation Works

### Basic Rule

**You cannot reserve the same desk twice in a row.**

The system tracks your **last active reservation** and blocks you from selecting that desk number for your next booking.

### What Counts as Your "Last Reservation"

The system looks at your **most recent active reservation** before the date you're trying to book:

- ✅ **Counts**: Confirmed reservations (`estado = true`)
- ✅ **Counts**: Pending reservations that were not canceled (`estado = null`)
- ❌ **Does not count**: Canceled reservations (`estado = false`)
- ❌ **Does not count**: Auto-canceled reservations ("! Usuario no confirmo!")

### Example Scenarios

**Scenario 1: Simple Rotation**

| Date | Desk | Status | Can Book Desk 3? |
|------|------|--------|------------------|
| March 10 | Desk 3 | Confirmada | — |
| March 11 | — | — | ❌ No (must choose 1, 2, 4, 5, or 6) |
| March 11 | Desk 1 | Confirmada | — |
| March 12 | — | — | ✅ Yes (last was Desk 1) |

**Scenario 2: Cancellation Resets Rotation**

| Date | Desk | Status | Notes |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| March 10 | Desk 6 | Confirmada | Last active reservation |
| March 11 | Desk 6 | Cancelada | Canceled by user |
| March 12 | — | — | ❌ Cannot book Desk 6 (last active is still March 10) |

**Scenario 3: Auto-Cancellation Allows Retry**

| Date | Desk | Status | Notes |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| March 10 | Desk 1 | Confirmada | — |
| March 11 | Desk 2 | Cancelada (auto) | "! Usuario no confirmo!" |
| March 12 | — | — | ❌ Cannot book Desk 1 (last active) |
| March 12 | — | — | ✅ Can book Desk 2 (auto-canceled doesn't count) |

## Rotation Validation Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="System Fetches Your History">
    When you open the reservation screen, the system queries your past reservations:
    
    ```
    GET /api/working-reservas?
      filters[documento][$eq]=[your_document]
      &filters[fecha_reserva][$lt]=[selected_date]
      &sort[0]=fecha_reserva:desc
    ```
    
    This retrieves up to **50 past reservations** sorted by date (most recent first).
  </Step>

  <Step title="System Identifies Last Active Desk">
    The system scans your history to find the **first active reservation** (not canceled):
    
    - Checks `estado !== false` (not canceled)
    - Checks `estado !== 'Cancelada'`
    - Extracts the `puestoId` (desk number) from that reservation
    
    This becomes your **`ultimoPuestoReservado`** (last reserved desk).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Visual Indicator on Map">
    The desk map applies a **grayscale filter** to the blocked desk:
    
    ```javascript
    filter: 'grayscale(0.55) brightness(0.9)'
    ```
    
    Hover tooltip shows:
    
    **"Rotación activa: elige un escritorio distinto al [desk number]"**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Blocking During Selection">
    If you click the blocked desk, the system displays an error:
    
    **"Tu última reserva fue el [date] en el escritorio [number]. Debes hacer rotación y elegir otro escritorio."**
    
    Example:
    - "Tu última reserva fue el 14/03/2026 en el escritorio 3. Debes hacer rotación y elegir otro escritorio."
    
    The booking card **does not open** for this desk.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Server-Side Validation">
    Even if you bypass the UI (unlikely), the server validates rotation rules before saving:
    
    - If `puestoId` matches your last active desk → **Reservation rejected**
    - Error message returned to client
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Error Messages

### "Debes elegir un escritorio distinto al de tu última reserva."

**When**: The system couldn't determine your last desk number (data issue).

**What to do**: Choose any desk **except the one you remember using last**.

---

### "Tu última reserva fue el [DD/MM/YYYY] en el escritorio [N]. Debes hacer rotación y elegir otro escritorio."

**When**: You clicked on the desk you used in your last active reservation.

**What to do**: Select a **different desk number** (1–6, excluding the blocked one).

**Example**:
- Last reservation: March 14, Desk 3
- Blocked: Desk 3
- Available: Desks 1, 2, 4, 5, 6

---

### "Validando regla de rotación..."

**When**: The system is fetching your reservation history (loading state).

**What to do**: Wait a few seconds for validation to complete. The message will disappear, and you can proceed.

---

### "Aún estamos validando la rotación de puestos. Intenta de nuevo en unos segundos."

**When**: You tried to confirm a reservation while rotation data was still loading.

**What to do**: Wait and retry. This usually resolves in 2–3 seconds.

### What If All Available Desks Are Full?

If rotation restricts you to 5 desks, and all 5 are fully booked:

1. **Wait for your last desk to become available** (if you canceled it or it auto-canceled)
2. **Book a different shift** on the blocked desk (if only one shift is taken)
3. **Choose another date** when more desks are available
4. **Contact an admin** if there's a legitimate need for override

<Tip>
  **Book early in the day** to have the widest selection of rotation-compliant desks.
</Tip>

## Rotation and Cancellations

### Manual Cancellation

If you **manually cancel** a reservation:

- The cancellation **does not reset** the rotation tracker
- Your last **active** (non-canceled) reservation still applies

**Example**:
1. March 10: Desk 5 (Confirmada) ← **Last active**
2. March 11: Desk 3 (Cancelada by user)
3. March 12: **Cannot book Desk 5** (last active is still March 10)

### Auto-Cancellation ("! Usuario no confirmo!")

If a reservation **auto-cancels** due to missed confirmation:

- The cancellation **does reset** rotation for that desk
- You can book the same desk again next time

**Example**:
1. March 10: Desk 2 (Confirmada) ← **Last active**
2. March 11: Desk 2 (Cancelada - auto) ← Missed confirmation
3. March 12: **Cannot book Desk 2** (last active is still March 10)
4. March 13: **Can book Desk 2** (if March 12 was a different desk)

**Why**: Auto-cancellation means you **didn't physically use** the desk, so it doesn't count toward occupancy rotation.

## Admin Override

Administrators are **exempt** from the rotation policy:

- Admins can book the **same desk consecutively**
- No rotation validation occurs for admin accounts
- Identified by document numbers in the `ADMINS` array:
  - Example: `'1028783377'`

<Note>
  If you're an admin and see rotation errors, verify your document number is correctly configured in the system.
</Note>

## Technical Details (For Developers)

### Rotation Check Code

```javascript
const buildUserHistoryUrl = (documento, fecha) => {
  const params = new URLSearchParams();
  params.set('filters[documento][$eq]', documento);
  params.set('filters[fecha_reserva][$lt]', fecha);
  params.set('sort[0]', 'fecha_reserva:desc');
  params.set('pagination[pageSize]', '50');
  params.set('populate', '*');
  return `${API_RESERVAS}?${params.toString()}`;
};
```

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does rotation apply within the same day?

**No.** You can only make **one reservation per day**, so rotation only affects **subsequent days**.

### What if I canceled my last reservation?

**Manual cancellation** does not reset rotation. The system looks at the last **active** (non-canceled) reservation before that.

### Can I request a rotation exception?

**For regular users**: No, the system enforces rotation automatically.

**For special needs**: Contact an administrator to discuss accessibility or equipment requirements.

### How far back does the system check?

**Up to 50 past reservations**, sorted by date. In practice, it finds your most recent active reservation.

### Does rotation apply to different rooms?

**Yes.** If the system has multiple rooms, rotation applies **per desk within all rooms**, not per room.

Example:
- Room A, Desk 3 (last reservation)
- Room B, Desk 3 → **Blocked** (same desk number, even though different room)

<Warning>
  This behavior depends on how desks are numbered across rooms in the system configuration.
</Warning>

### What if the rotation system has an error?

**Symptoms**:
- All desks appear blocked
- Error message shows "Validando regla de rotación..." indefinitely
- You know you didn't use a desk, but it's blocked

**Solutions**:
1. **Refresh the page** – Clears the loading state
2. **Check your reservation history** in the User Panel – Verify your last active reservation
3. **Contact support** – Provide your document number and the error message

## Summary

The rotation policy ensures **fair, transparent, and automated** desk distribution:

1. **Rule**: Cannot book the same desk twice in a row
2. **Tracker**: Last **active** (non-canceled) reservation determines the blocked desk
3. **Validation**: UI blocks clicks + server rejects invalid requests
4. **Errors**: Clear messages show which desk is blocked and why
5. **Exemptions**: Admins bypass rotation
6. **Benefits**: Fair access, no monopolization, transparent enforcement

  **Plan your week ahead** by rotating between your preferred desks. If you need monitors, alternate between Desks 1, 3, and 6.
</Tip>
